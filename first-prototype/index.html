  • High Concept: This is a game let player to control the jet, aviod the bullet sot by the alien jet and attack the all the alien jets.
  • Theme: In 2077, the aliens from a star far away is trying to occupy Earth, as the guardian of the earth, you have to protect our planet!
  • Mandated Variety: The main control of the game is the keyboard, use up, down, left and right to control the jet fly, and use space to fire, player can also click on the pause button to pause the game and unpause by click it again. The bullet shot by the enemy is randomly, player should aviod the attack.
  • Prototype Goal: To build the main part of the whole game. Start from this frototype, the rest prototypes and the whole game will be multiple checkpoints, and become more and more difficult.
  • Player Experience Goals: Feels satisfied when distroy the enemy or clean up all the enemies.
  • Gameplay: This is a game which let player attack the enemies. When the game start, player will have three chances(live) to kill all the enemies. Each enemy killed will give player 20 points. Once all the enemies have been killed, the player will have 1000 extra points. The points will be useful for the future game use. The control of the game is the keyborad and mouse. Player can use up, down, left and right to control the jet, and use space on the keyboard to fire. Player also can use the mouse to click on the pause button to pause the game.
  • Strategies: The main player strategy is focus on more than the player itself(it is the jet in the game), and also see the enemies' fire.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The main story setting is in the nearly future. In 2045, the aliens from a star far away is trying to occupy Earth, as the guardian of the earth, player have to protect our planet!
  • Target Audience: Any one trying to have fun by attack the enemy and survive from attack.
  • Play Time: From one minute up to as long as user want to.
